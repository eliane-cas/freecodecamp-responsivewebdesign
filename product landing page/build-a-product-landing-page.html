About Us

Here's the deal: we're big on keeping it local. Our fruit doesn't come from some far-off farm – it's grown right here in our community's allotments. We've hooked up with nearby growers who know their stuff, so when you snag one of our fruit baskets, you're getting the freshest produce straight from our backyard. It's all about supporting local and tasting the difference!
